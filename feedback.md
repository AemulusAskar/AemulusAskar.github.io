# Contact & Feedback

If you encountered a bug, want to share your thoughts, or have a business inquiry, feel free to contact me. 

To help me notice your message faster and prevent it from getting lost in spam, please use one of the templates below or write to `aemulusaskar@gmail.com` and make sure your email subject starts with **`[Aemulus Askar]`**.
